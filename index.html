DocPlain AI helps you understand and generate common legal documents, but it is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. For complex legal matters, we always recommend consulting a licensed US attorney.
Are the documents legally valid in all US states? +
Our templates are designed to comply with US federal law and are customizable per state. We cover all 50 US states. Always verify documents for your specific jurisdiction before signing.

Absolutely. All your documents are encrypted with 256-bit AES encryption. We never sell or share your legal data with any third parties. You own your documents 100%.

Yes! On Pro and Business plans, you can upload any PDF contract and our AI will analyze it, flag risky clauses, and summarize key terms in plain English.
